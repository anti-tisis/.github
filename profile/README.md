## About Us

We are **Anti-Tisis**, a dedicated team of Computer Science students from the **University of Cabuyao**. Our collective is composed of diverse talents united by a single purpose: to push the boundaries of technology. 

We are not just students; we are builders, researchers, and creators. Our team specializes in a wide array of cutting-edge disciplines, fostering an environment where innovation thrives through cross-disciplinary collaboration.
